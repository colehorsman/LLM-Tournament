**Non-Human Identities Will Dominate the Cybersecurity Landscape in 2026—Are You Prepared?**

By 2026, the ratio of Non-Human Identities (NHIs) to human identities in cloud-native enterprises will surge to an alarming 100:1, and in environments heavily reliant on AI agents, this could skyrocket to 500:1. This isn't just a statistic; it's a wake-up call for security leaders.

As NHIs proliferate, they will become prime targets for attackers. A compromised service account or API token can lead to catastrophic breaches, as these entities often possess excessive privileges that go unchecked for far too long. In response, organizations must implement rigorous identity security measures, starting with a comprehensive audit of current privileges. 

Here’s a checklist to get you started:

1. **Audit NHIs:** Use tools like AWS IAM Access Analyzer or Azure Privileged Identity Management to assess existing service accounts and their permissions.
2. **Implement Zero Standing Privilege (ZSP):** Transition to a ZSP model where all identities, human or non-human, are granted permissions only when necessary. Consider using tools like CyberArk or BeyondTrust to manage this.
3. **Establish Behavioral Baselines:** Implement monitoring systems that can identify deviations in behavior for NHIs, potentially flagging misuse before it leads to a breach.

As you plan for 2026, ask yourself: What steps are you taking today to secure your NHIs against tomorrow's threats? Let's discuss how we can better prepare for this evolving landscape.
